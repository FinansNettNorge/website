About FNN

FinansNettNorge AS (FNN) was established on 18 June 2004.

Main Objectives

  • To ensure user‑managed control of the communication network related to trading and settlement of financial instruments.
  • To help ensure simple and inexpensive connection of external services.
  • To ensure a network with sufficient capacity and robustness for backup and business continuity.

Network Operations

The company has entered into an agreement with NetNordic Norway AS on the delivery and operation of the network as well as support functions. The body of agreement with NetNordic Norway AS ensures that the network at all times meets the requirements stipulated by Finanstilsynet (The Financial Supervisory Authority of Norway) in the form of the ICT Regulations and that Finanstilsynet is ensured the necessary access for its supervision work.

The network is approved as a provider of solutions for communicating with Euronext trading systems (ie Oslo Stock Exchange), including MultiCast distribution.

International Agreements

FNN has also entered into agreements on communication solutions that also cover communication with service providers established in countries other than Norway.

Technology Infrastructure

All FNN's communication technology is being renewed in 2025/26 and is based on IP-Fabric technology from Extreme Networks.
